## Matter Purification Loop (Executive Overview)

The loop transitions contaminated agricultural runoff into neutralized soil conditions using a Hemp/Clay phytoremediation cycle.

- Phase 1: Biological uptake and binding within a controlled loop.
- Phase 2: Stabilization via clay-mediated adsorption (non-reactive containment).
- Phase 3: Regenerated growth media supports repeatable remediation cycles.
- Phase 4: Output pathway converts stabilized biomass into scalable Hempcrete structural materials.

This is presented as a public-good mechanism: repeatable, scalable, and privacy-safe.
