# Governance

## Steward’s Privacy is Absolute

- No external network dependency for access.
- No location disclosure beyond approved aliases.
- No write actions from this interface.

---

## Neutrality Protocol (Sponsor Boundary)

- Sponsors gain access to the discovery pathway and evidence under agreed terms.
- Sponsors do not dictate ethical boundaries.
- The Me-We Authority is non-negotiable: public-good impact without coercion.

## Immutable Gold Master

- There is a single authoritative reference build (the Immutable Gold Master).
- All sponsored variants must remain ABI-compatible and privacy-compliant.
- No silent instrumentation, telemetry, or hidden network calls.

## Sponsor Participation (Read-Only)

- Bronze / Gold / Founding Steward engagement is advisory and resourcing-focused.
- Decisions remain auditable.
- Outputs remain deterministic and local-first.

## Operating Principles

- Determinism over improvisation
- Auditability over ambiguity
- Accessibility over speed
